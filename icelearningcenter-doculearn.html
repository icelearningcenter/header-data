Important Information for Using DocuLearn

Be sure to save your work often as you go by clicking on the Save Progress button at the top. To be extra careful, save your work after completing each section.

DocuLearn works best on Chrome.

If using a Mac, disable the "swipe between pages" feature under Apple System Preferences/Trackpad, as this might cause you to unintentionally back out of the DocuLearn webpage when you swipe left/right and lose your work.
